WEIRD DOCTORS

High Quality 3D Animated NFT's on cardano!

Hello there!
Weird Doctors is a 3D-animation art project expanding into a playable game.
It is founded by 3D-artist Bob Joostens who is putting his heart and soul into this project.
Trying to deliver an awesome gameplay and tradeable NFT collection
with rarity, multiple 3D characters and much more
to make good use of the available options that web3 and the NFT world have to offer.

The goals of this project

1

Raise the bar in the world of NFT art.

How?

Each NFT will have:
- A unique combination of many traits.
- Detailed materials and textures.
- Multiple fully rigged 3D-animated characters.
- Manually brought to life with an eye for the 12 principles of animation.
- A place in a visually appealing world with doctors acting weird.

Why?

Aren't you tired of seeing these projects popping up everywhere having the same easy-to-make illustrations??
I am. I want to spend time and put effort in making the art stand out.

2

Try to help people in danger.

How?

By donating 20% of profit to organisations that save lives.

Why?

Like many of you, we believe in the power of helping people too.
If this project succeeds we want to make sure it has a positive impact on other people as well.

3

Make a fun game to play together!

How?

By buying our animated art you support the development of our very own
3D multiplayer platformer game.
The goal is to make it so you can connect your cardano wallet and use the animations from the NFTs you own to play the game.
Every collection will have a specific animation which will represent your player's medical approach in the process of saving lives.

Why?

Because we want this project to be more than just animated artwork.
We want to build this into a community full of people having fun playing a game together.
We want to give the owners of the animations the ability to put their weird doctors to good use!
