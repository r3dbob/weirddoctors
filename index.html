WEIRD DOCTORS

High Quality 3D Animated NFT's on cardano!

Hello there!
My name is Bob. I am an artist specialised in 3D-production.
This is my NFT-project called Weird Doctors.

The goals of this project

1

Raise the bar in the world of NFT art.

How?

Each NFT will have:
- A unique combination of many traits.
- Detailed materials and textures.
- Multiple fully rigged 3D-animated characters.
- Manually brought to life with an eye for the 12 principles of animation.
- A place in a visually appealing world with doctors acting weird.

Why?

Aren't you tired of seeing these projects popping up everywhere having the same easy-to-make illustrations??
I am. I want to spend time and put effort in making the art stand out.

2

Try to help people in danger.

How?

By donating 20% of profit to organisations that save lives.

Why?

Like many of you, I believe in the power of helping people too.
If this project succeeds I want to make sure it has a positive impact on other people as well.

3

Touching people with visual art.

How?

Releasing a series of short animated episodes.
The goal of this series is to support people who feel different and encourage them to stay weird.
Each episode will focus on something that causes people to feel lonely/disconnected.

Why?

I think it is important to not only support physical health but also mental health.
